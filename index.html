ABOUT

ABOUT

Hello, my name is Thomas Lee Flick, the creator of the technology firm Option Alternative and self-taught software developer from Cincinnati, OH. I would say that my style is somewhat unorthodox compared to most web designers and web developers out there. I'm also a musician and try to apply how i write songs to design/development so it becomes less stale and more unique..

I've never went to college, but i have an ambition and thirst to create amazing design concepts and interstellar products. I have a work hard/play hard philosophy,but i try to keep a good balance. My work ethic should speak for itself as im hard headed about ways i like to do things and am a extreme independent thinker. If you have patience and your business, whether it be big or small wants a superb product, then look no further!!/
